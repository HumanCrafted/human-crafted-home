## Recipe (single batch, ~10 golf ball-sized bites)

- 1 cup old fashioned oats
- ¼ cup peanut butter
- ¼ cup honey
- ½ cup roasted salted peanuts
- ½ tsp vanilla (optional)

Mix all ingredients, roll into golf ball-sized portions. Double batch = ~20 bites.
